ZestyNickel

'ZestyNickel' a.k.a Daniel, is a part-time streamer on Twitch.TV. He plays a variety of games with a large focus on games such as Super Smash Bros. and other Nintendo titles, one big one being Mario Kart. If any of that interests you, check him out!

Ongoing

//mission

Creating a cohesive visual brand to represent Daniel and his Twitch channel 'ZestyNickel' to help create a definite distinction between himself and others.

//problem

Entering into the project, I was given full creative control. Fairly unproblematic, but finding a style that suited the channel was much tougher than initially anticipated.

//process

I had a meeting with Daniel over Discord to determine what about his previous overlay he wanted changed. The eventual answer came to everything, so I provided him with some examples and we established what he was looking for. With that, I got to work. Being given full creative control after the meeting concluded was tough, but I think it led to a cohesive and interesting final product.
