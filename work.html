STRESS


Have you ever felt like the weight of the world was resting on your shoulders? Have you ever felt like everything was against you, and that before you had a chance to get a grip on the situation, that the other side was going to pull you farther and farther into darkness. That the stresses of life would eventually catch up to you? As part of a project, during a overwhelmingly stressful period in my life, I felt the best way to combat the stress was to create a video visualizing what stress feels like, at least from a personal perspective.

Tools

Paper and Pencil
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Premiere Pro
Adobe After Effects

Duration

6 weeks
