If you've ever felt like the weight of the world was on your shoulders, and was getting heavier and heavier by the day, eventually becoming too heavy to bear, then this will make sense. The project is a motion design piece that was designed to combine motion and illustrative elements with real footage. I wanted to represent stress in a way that feels more accurate to my interpretation, so in doing that, I used a very distinct style, filled with grunge-y textures and glitch effects to get the tone of being stressed across.

Tools

Paper and Pencil
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Premiere Pro
Adobe After Effects
