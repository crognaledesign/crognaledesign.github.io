216Duck

'Duck' is a gamer and part-time streamer on Twitch.TV. He plays a variety of games with his friends, and from time to time watches people make food. If any of that interests you, check him out!



Tools

Paper and Pencil
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe After Effects

Roles

Art Direction
Graphic Design
Motion Design

Duration

2 Weeks
Completed

The Mission

Creating a cohesive visual brand to represent 'Duck' and his Twitch channel to separate his channel from the rest

The Problem

Entering into the project blind, with minimal direction, trying to create an end product that satisfies the wants of the client

The Process

First, I met with Duck to get an idea of what he was looking for before I got started with anything. The overall process was largely up to me with very specific points with regards to a general style. He spoke about a general style, rough and edgy, with a simple and clean finish (A bit difficult to explain without visual reference) and a simple 2 colour mix (+ black). The end result was far from minimal, but encapsulates the simple and clean, yet rough and punchy look, and from there the project came together with relative ease.
