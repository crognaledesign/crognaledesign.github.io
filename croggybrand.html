Croggy

I have been streaming on twitch as a hobby for the last eight years or so, but over the last couple of years, I've been struggling with whether to abandon my personal brand or not, and since I launched this website, and subsequently my professional brand, I decided to split my brand in two. My professional brand, Fulmine, and my personal hobbyist brand, Croggy, a play on my last name. Fun fact, Croggy is the british word for taking a bike ride on somebody elses handlebars!

//process

I wanted to achieve a very particular style with my new personal hobbyist brand revamp. The first version of this branding was red and orange, but with a very edgy, rough visual style. I liked it, but felt it was lacking some personality. This time around, the logo has been hand-lettered, and then slightly altered and perfected in Illustrator, and I used my love of music, audio and motion to bring everything together, as well as some Hiragana characters, because I love not only the visual representation of them as symbols, but have a strange love for Japanese culture.
