//manic

Relating to or affected by Mania

"the manic interludes the depression"

Showing wild, apparently deranged, excitement and energy.

"a manic grin"

Based on the definition of Manic, as part of an experimental type oriented motion project, I wanted to try to visually represent the inner turmoil expressed in the above definitions. In two versions of the same piece, I have explored two totally different visalization methods for the word Manic




//tools

Paper and Pencil
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Premiere Pro
Adobe After Effects

//duration

This project is currently a WIP and is not indicative of the final product.

//process

Experimentation began in After Effects. Storyboards were done in ProCreate after digital experimentation.

Then I made a list of characters, colours, visual styles and made note of their tonal differences to emulate a future-grunge style.





//version 2

Believe it or not, version 2 was created first. At least, the digital version was, however, the storyboard for version 1 was created mere hours before I began the digital process.

Whereby the first version was built upon concepts of distortion and the visual representation of the 'Wild and deranged' mentality, this version, Version 2, was built on visualizing the excited and energetic part of the definition. I wanted to express both, and am currently working on developing a way to combine both approaches.
