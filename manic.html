In today's world, esports are just as popular as traditional sports, in some aspects even more popular. The purpose behind this project was to design an esports brand and take the time to build something from the ground up that all parties involved could be proud of. Something that represents not only the fun, vibrant tone generally associated with video games, but also something that stands strong and powerful.

Tools

Paper and Pencil
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Premiere Pro
Adobe After Effects

Duration

Ongoing







Creating a video to show the new brand identity was an interesting task. Specifically with regards to the stream elements. Most screens such as the Starting Soon Screen and Be Right Back screen needed to visually match the rest of the brand, but there are static elements that should be animated. However due to the nature of Twitch streams, that's an are of disconnect with the rest of the indentity in which sacrifices needed to be made.
