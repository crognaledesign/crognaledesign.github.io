//manic

Relating to or affected by Mania

"the manic interludes the depression"

Showing wild, apparently deranged, excitement and energy.

"a manic grin"


Based on the definition of Manic, as part of an experimental type oriented motion project, I wanted to try to visually represent the inner turmoil expressed in the above definitions. In two versions of the same piece, I have explored two totally different visalization methods for the word Manic

Tools

Paper and Pencil
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Premiere Pro
Adobe After Effects

Duration

This project is currently a
work-in-progress and is not indicative of the final product.

Process

Experimentation began in After Effects. Storyboards were transferred to ProCreate after
digital experimentation.

Even before I started working in
After Effects, I researched the
word and began to make a list of characters, colours, visual styles
and made note of their tonal differences.

The video was designed to
emulate a future-grunge style.





Creating a video to show the new brand identity was an interesting task. Specifically with regards to the stream elements. Most screens such as the Starting Soon Screen and Be Right Back screen needed to visually match the rest of the brand, but there are static elements that should be animated. However due to the nature of Twitch streams, that's an are of disconnect with the rest of the indentity in which sacrifices needed to be made.
